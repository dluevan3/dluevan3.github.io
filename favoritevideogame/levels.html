Playthrough

Chapter 1: Hung Over

Setting: Begins in a surreal tavern and transitions to the hub world, Windy.
Plot: Conker wakes up with a massive hangover and must navigate basic platforming challenges.
Notables: The tutorial introduces Berri (his girlfriend), Birdy the scarecrow and the Panther King.
Enemies: Basic foes like dung beetles and wasps.

Chapter 2: Windy

Setting: A lush countryside hub with multiple branching paths.
Plot: Conker helps various oddball characters, including a sunflower who needs pollination and a bee with a cheating wife.
Notables: The Great Mighty Poo is teased here.
Enemies: Wasps, pitchforks, and hay bales.

Chapter 3: Barn Boys

Setting: A barn filled with talking farm equipment.
Plot: Conker aids a suicidal pitchfork named Franky and battles a haybot.
Boss: Haybot – a malfunctioning robot made of hay and metal.

Chapter 4: Bats Tower

Setting: A gothic tower infested with bats.
Plot: Conker must retrieve money and navigate tricky platforming.
Enemies: Bats and vampire creatures.
Boss: No major boss, but the level is puzzle-heavy.

Chapter 5: Sloprano

Setting: A sewer system filled with filth.
Plot: Conker must defeat a singing pile of feces.
Boss: The Great Mighty Poo – an opera-singing dung monster who hurls feces while belting out arias.

Chapter 6: Uga Buga

Setting: Prehistoric jungle and tribal village.
Plot: Conker joins a caveman tribe and rides a raptor.
Boss: Buga the Knut – a brutish caveman who must be tricked into falling.

Chapter 7: Spooky

Setting: A haunted mansion.
Plot: Conker becomes a vampire bat and must feed villagers to a blood pool.
Enemies: Zombies, skeletons, and ghosts.
Boss: Count Batula – Conker’s vampiric alter ego.

Chapter 8: It's War

Setting: A gritty battlefield parodying WWII.
Plot: Conker joins the Squirrel army to fight the Tediz (evil teddy bears).
Boss: Tediz Commander – a mechanized bear with heavy artillery.

Chapter 9: The Heist

Setting: A bank vault and Matrix-style lobby.
Plot: Conker and a mobster team up to rob a bank.
Boss: Mecha Panther King – a robotic version of the Panther King piloted by Professor Von Kriplespac.
